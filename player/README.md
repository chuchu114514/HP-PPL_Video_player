How to use：Just copy your handled frames to there and drag the whole folder into your calculator
Enter the app named "视频" and press "view" and play your video
You can press "Esc" to stop playing the video and press any others to start.
Tips：frames should be named with structures like 001.jpg （not 1.jpg）, or it may not work as you think

进入“视频”软件然后按下"view"以开始播放
你可以按“Esc”以暂停播放并按其他任意键以开始
